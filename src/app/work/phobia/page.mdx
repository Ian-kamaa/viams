## Requirements for UK skilled worker visa / COS
<ul class="list-disc">
  <li>Job Offer: You must have a job offer from a UK employer who is a licensed sponsor.</li>
  <li>Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS): Your employer must provide you with a valid CoS, which contains details about the job you’ve been offered in the UK.</li>
  <li>Skill Level: The job must be at or above the required skill level (RQF level 3 or equivalent).</li>
  <li>Salary: You must be paid a minimum salary, which is usually at least £26,200 per year or the ‘going rate’ for the job, whichever is higher.</li>
  <li>English Language Proficiency: You must demonstrate a certain level of English language proficiency, typically B1 on the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) scale.</li>
  <li>Financial Requirement: You must have sufficient funds to support yourself without relying on public funds unless your employer can certify maintenance.</li>
  <li>TB Test: If you’re from a listed country, you may need to provide a tuberculosis (TB) test result.</li>

</ul>
