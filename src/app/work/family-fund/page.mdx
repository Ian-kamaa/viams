## Requirements for Visitor Visa Applications

<ul class="list-none">
  <li>Valid passport with a minimum validity of six months</li>
  <li>Two passport size photographs that are clear and high quality</li>
  <li>A bank statement showing sufficient funds to cover your travel expenses during</li>
  <li>Purpose of Travel: A letter explaining why you are visiting Canada.</li>
  <li>Travel History: Copies of visas and entry/exit stamps for countries you have visited.</li>
  <li>Biometrics: Depending on your country of residence, you may need to provide fingerprints and a photo.</li>
  <li>Application Fees: The fee is CAD $100 per person, plus an additional CAD $85 for biometrics if required</li>
</ul>
